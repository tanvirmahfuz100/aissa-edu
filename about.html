About Tanvir Mahfuz

Bridging accounting foundation with advanced data analysis.

Tanvir Mahfuz is a BBA student in Accounting & Information Systems at the University of Barishal. Passionate about determining the intersection of finance and technology, he works to combine traditional accounting principles with modern data analysis techniques.

With competency in Microsoft Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI, he focuses on prototyping practical solutions that address real-world challenges for academic and professional communities.
